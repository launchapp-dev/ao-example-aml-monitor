# SAR Narrative Template

> This template guides the structure of a FinCEN SAR narrative.
> Replace all [BRACKETED] placeholders with specific, factual information.
> Do not include legal conclusions or speculation. Be specific with dates, amounts, and account numbers.
> Target length: 400-800 words.

---

## WHO

[Subject full legal name] ([SSN/TIN or "whose SSN is unknown"]), a [individual/entity] with address at [full address], holds [account type] account number [XXXX] at [Institution Name], [branch location]. [Subject] has been a customer since [date] and is [occupation/business type].

[If additional subjects]: Also involved is [second subject name], who [relationship to first subject — e.g., "holds account XXXX at the same institution" or "serves as the registered agent for [Entity Name], which received wire transfers from the subject"].

## WHAT

[Institution Name] identified suspicious activity involving [general description — e.g., "a pattern of structured cash deposits, multiple wire transfers through shell companies, and rapid movement of funds inconsistent with the customer's stated business purpose"].

The activity involves a total of approximately $[cumulative amount] in suspicious transactions conducted between [start date] and [end date].

Specifically: [describe the activity in concrete terms — what types of transactions, what amounts, what counterparties, in what sequence. Be precise.]

Example: "Between [date] and [date], the subject made [N] cash deposits to account [XXXX] totaling $[amount]. The deposits ranged from $[low] to $[high], with [N] deposits falling between $9,000 and $9,900. Following each deposit, the subject wired the funds to [counterparty name] at [bank name] within [timeframe]."

## WHEN

The suspicious activity was first detected on [detection date] through [how — automated monitoring, teller referral, etc.]. The activity spans from [earliest transaction date] through [most recent transaction date], a period of [X] days/months.

[If applicable]: This report covers the period [start date] through [end date]. A prior SAR was filed on [date] under BSA ID [number], and this report reflects continuation of that suspicious activity.

## WHERE

[If wire transfers]: Funds were transferred to/from accounts at [counterparty institutions], located in [cities/states/countries]. [If offshore]: Funds passed through financial institutions in [countries], jurisdictions identified by FATF as [grey-listed/high-risk].
